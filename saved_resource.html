Realidade Virtual Imersiva

 

 

Immersive Virtual Reality

Computer Science Graduation Thesis presented as a partial requisite to the obtantion of Computer Science degree from  Centro de Ensino Superior do Pará - CESUPA- Brazil, year1999
Trabalho de Conclusão de Curso apresentado como requisito parcial à obtenção do grau de Tecnólogo em Processamento de Dados pelo Centro de Ensino Superior do Pará - CESUPA no ano de 1999

 

Abstract

         Taking into account the development of the Virtual Reality in the last years, this work presents the main forms of RV, considering its participation in the creation of immersives experiences, that is the main purpose of this work. In this way, it is necessary to present respectively, among other, the notions of Cyberspace,  Cybertime, Space and Absolute Time that allow a general vision of the area.  Following, the several immersives forms of RV, we uttered, also, the forms of Telepresence, Hypertext and Cyborgs, proposing them as a new interface paradigm with the user. Through examples, the existent applications of RV are focused in several areas of the knowledge.   Therefore, the present work counts with a vast bibliographical resource obtained through several research forms (Internet, Books, Centers of Research and Development, Published Works and Lectures), which, in a certain way, makes to stand out its importance as source for consultation.
